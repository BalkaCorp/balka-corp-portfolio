About us

We are creative graphic designers and video editors. We specialize in logo design, banners, and website development. With a passion for combining aesthetics with functionality, we strive to create visually stunning and user-friendly designs that capture the essence of every project.

At Bałka Corporation, we believe in the power of strong visual identity. Every pixel we create is carefully crafted to deliver not just beauty, but purpose. Our experience ranges from working with small businesses to international brands, bringing a personal touch and strategic thinking to every design.

We are constantly evolving and staying up to date with the latest trends in design and technology. Our aim is to build long-term relationships with our clients based on trust, creativity, and results that exceed expectations.
